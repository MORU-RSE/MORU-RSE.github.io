Who We Are

A New RSE Team at the Heart of MORU

The MORU RSE Group is a research software engineering team embedded within the Mahidol Oxford Tropical Medicine Research Unit. We work alongside scientists and clinicians to design, develop, and maintain the software that underpins modern tropical medicine research.

We are a young team — but that means we bring fresh perspectives, modern practices, and genuine enthusiasm to every project we take on. From day one, we are committed to writing software that is open, reproducible, and built to last.

Software Engineering Clean, tested, maintainable code
Reproducibility Open science from the ground up
Global Health Impact Software that serves researchers worldwide

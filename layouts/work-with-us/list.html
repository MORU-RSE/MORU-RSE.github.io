Our Process

What to Expect

A typical collaboration follows four stages. We keep things lightweight and researcher-friendly throughout.

1

Initial Conversation

We start with a short meeting to understand your project, your data, and what you need the software to do. No technical knowledge required on your end — just tell us the science.

2

Scoping & Planning

We produce a short scoping document — outlining the proposed approach, technology choices, timeline, and effort required. This becomes the basis of our agreement.

3

Build & Iterate

We develop the software in short cycles, sharing progress regularly and adjusting based on your feedback. You stay involved without needing to attend every technical meeting.

4

Handover & Support

We document everything, run training sessions where needed, and ensure the code lives in a place your team can maintain and build on long after our engagement ends.
